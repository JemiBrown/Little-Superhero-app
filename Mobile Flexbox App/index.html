Little Superhero

The little boy who saved my life. The birthday gift from God. The tiny soul that brought me back from the darkest depth I have ever known.

You are a force, a life changer, unstoppable, and one of the most amazing things in my world. Everyone who knows you, loves you.

We are breaking generational curses together, we are healing mother wounds and father wounds and going to leave this world better than we found it.

You are so small but you have already changed this world just as God intended. I love you. I am forever rejoicing and praising the Lord that I get to be your mom.

You are my little SuperHero in disguise. I want to build a life around you that is deserving of you. Give you everything you gave me and more.
